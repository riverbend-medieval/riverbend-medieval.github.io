Medieval Re-enactment in Queensland

Queensland is home to a vibrant community of medieval re-enactment groups, each offering unique experiences in historical interpretation, combat, crafts, and cultural activities. From the bustling cities of Brisbane and Gold Coast to the scenic regions of the Fraser Coast and beyond, medieval enthusiasts can find groups dedicated to bringing history to life.

This directory connects medieval re-enactment enthusiasts with local groups across Queensland, making it easier to discover events, join societies, and participate in the rich medieval heritage that thrives throughout our state.

Medieval Events Across Queensland

Queensland hosts numerous medieval events throughout the year, including:

  • Medieval Fairs: Various locations across Queensland
  • Historical Festivals: Annual events featuring medieval re-enactment
  • Combat Demonstrations: Regular training and public displays
  • Craft Workshops: Medieval skills and traditional crafts
  • Educational Programs: School visits and historical education

How to Get Involved

Interested in joining a medieval re-enactment group in Queensland?

  1. Research Groups: Review the groups listed above and their specialties
  2. Attend Events: Visit public demonstrations and events to see groups in action
  3. Contact Groups: Reach out to groups that interest you
  4. Join ARA: Consider joining the Australian Reenactors Association for insurance and networking
  5. Start Small: Begin with basic equipment and gradually build your medieval persona
