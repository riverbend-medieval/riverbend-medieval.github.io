About Riverbend Medieval Society

Riverbend Medieval Society Inc. is a vibrant community dedicated to bringing medieval history to life through authentic re-enactment on the Fraser Coast, Queensland. We offer a unique opportunity to step back in time and experience the rich culture, traditions, and crafts of the medieval period from 800-1500 AD.

  1. To be a not for profit organisation to promote social and physical enactment for persons and their families for the purpose of promoting the historical era and lifestyles of persons living in England between the eras of 800 to 1500 AD.
  2. To promote and encourage tourism to the Fraser Coast.

The society is comprised of three sub-groups:

Our Sub-Groups

Sevenoaks Early Medieval Company
850–950 AD
The High Medieval Company
1100–1300 AD
The Companie of the Badger
