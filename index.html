About Us

Riverbend Medieval Society Inc. is a vibrant community dedicated to bringing medieval history to life through authentic re-enactment. We offer a unique opportunity to step back in time and experience the rich culture, traditions, and crafts of the medieval period.

  1. To be a not for profit organisation to promote social and physical enactment for persons and their families for the purpose of promoting the historical era and lifestyles of persons living in England between the eras of 800 to 1500 AD.
  2. To promote and encourage tourism to the Fraser Coast.

The society is comprised of three sub-groups:

Our Sub-Groups

  • Sevenoaks Early Medieval Company 850-950AD
  • The High Medieval Company 1100-1300AD
  • The Companie of the Badger 1350-1450AD
