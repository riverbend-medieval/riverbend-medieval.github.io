Basic Information

Name: Pierre du Pont

Period: Late Medieval (1350–1450)

Status: French noble and Olivine's companion

Pierre du Pont is a French nobleman who accompanies his sister Olivine on her adventures. While not a knight, he maintains the dignity and bearing of his noble birth, supporting his sister in her various endeavors.
